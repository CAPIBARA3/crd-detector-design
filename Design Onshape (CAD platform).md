# Design Onshape
Onshape is the CAD platform that we are using to design CapiCR.

If you want to access and contribute to the improvement of the current design, you may do so by following the next steps:
1. Create a student Onshape account: go to https://www.onshape.com/en/education/sign-up and fill the form.
2. Go to [here](https://cad.onshape.com/documents/b79e42eaa7cf39f4a762b033/w/d06a245f9d5383a108c11ec7/e/a0c05a11140fb503ba84c6e7?renderMode=0&uiState=68b9d7aebf2dc9d3e80e749e) to view the design,
and [here](https://cad.onshape.com/documents/3e1c7ca1bc19bf5ab525e65e/w/cadc6ff12fdf242ef4cfd395/e/52b1a09b4bb121e927d4dd17?renderMode=0&uiState=68b9d8f65acc127f25e69390) to view the assembly.
3. If you want to edit it, send me an email with the name of your account, and I'll grant you access.
